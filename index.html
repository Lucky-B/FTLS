FTLS Director:

Prof Emmanuel Mfanafuthi Mgqwashu is an Associate Professor and the current Director for Faculty Teaching & Support, Center for Teaching and Learning, North West University. He is the former Head of the Education Department at Rhodes University, having previously served as Deputy Dean within the Education Faculty.
He received his first National Research Foundation (NRF) Rating in 2019 in recognition of his top national research productivity that begins to enter the international arena. His scholarly work involves a co-investigator role in an NRF research project called Access and Inclusion in Higher Education with a focus on ways in which disciplinary discourses and pedagogic practices across disciplines in higher education serve to include and exclude students.
